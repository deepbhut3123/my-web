EcoShield Pro FIBC Bags

Home / EcoShield Pro FIBC Bags
New 2026 Collection Export Grade Custom Branding

Smart Bulk Packaging, Reimagined

EcoShield Pro FIBC is a next-generation jumbo bag series designed for modern logistics. It combines high load performance with cleaner visual branding so your product looks premium from warehouse to export dock.

Built using UV-stabilized woven polypropylene and precision-stitched loops, it is ideal for grains, minerals, fertilizer, polymers, and agro-products that need safe, stackable and moisture-resistant transport.
