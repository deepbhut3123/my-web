About Us

Founded in Vadodara, Gujarat, Amee Extrusion LLP is a manufacturer of precision aluminium collapsible tubes. We serve the pharmaceutical, cosmetic, food, and industrial sectors with consistently high-quality aluminium solutions.


Our state-of-the-art extrusion presses, paired with rigorous in-process quality checks, allow us to produce tubes with micron-level dimensional accuracy. We offer a wide range of diameters, volumes, and surface finishes.


What sets us apart is our ability to customise — whether it's a specific diameter, a specialised lacquer, or a printed surface. We engineer each tube to meet your exact application requirements.

How We Work

1. Material Sourcing

We source premium-grade aluminium alloys from certified suppliers, ensuring raw material integrity before a single billet enters our press.

2. Precision Extrusion

Our extrusion presses convert aluminium billets into tubes with micron-level dimensional accuracy, consistent wall thickness, and smooth internal surfaces.

3. Quality Inspection

Every batch undergoes rigorous dimensional, visual, and mechanical testing. Only tubes that meet our strict tolerances proceed to finishing.

4. Custom Finishing

Tubes are anodized, lacquered, printed, or colour-coated as per client specifications, then packed and dispatched with full quality documentation.
