About Us

Established in Vadodara, Gujarat, Amee Extrusion LLP is a leading manufacturer of high-quality aluminium collapsible tubes and precision extrusion profiles. With state-of-the-art machinery and a commitment to dimensional accuracy, we serve the pharmaceutical, cosmetic, food, and industrial sectors across India.

Our facility operates with strict quality control at every stage — from alloy selection and extrusion to surface finishing and dispatch — making us a preferred partner for brands that demand consistency and reliability.
